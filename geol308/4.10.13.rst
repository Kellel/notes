Surface Waves
-------------
 
  Love waves:
    
    - Slower than P or S
    - Oscillates side-to-side, like a snake
    - No vertical motion

  Rayleigh waves:
 
    - Slowest, most damaging
    - Oscillates in circular motion, like ocean waves
